Boulevardier

If you like a Negroni and you like whiskey, too, give this one a try. It's a whiskey Negroni. Play with the ratios; I sometimes pull back on the sweet vermouth just slightly. This is also great served on the rocks.

Ingredients

  • Whiskey / Bourbon - 1.5oz
  • Sweet Vermouth - 1oz
  • Campari - 1oz
  • Lemon Twist

Directions

  1. Fill glass with ice to chill
  2. Fill cocktail shaker with ice
  3. Add 1.5oz Whiskey or Bourbon to the shaker
  4. Add 1oz Sweet Vermouth to the shaker
  5. Add 1oz Campari or similar bitter orange apertif to the shaker
  6. Empty ice from chilling glass
  7. Stir contents and strain into chilled martini glass
  8. Garnish with a lemon twist
