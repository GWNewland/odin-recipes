Manhattan

An easy recipe for a Manhattan â€” a classic cocktail that is believed to date back to the late 1800s. It's made with a bracing mixture of bourbon or rye whiskey mixed with sweet vermouth and a dash of aromatic bitters.

Ingredients

  • Rye Whiskey - 2oz
  • Sweet Vermouth - .5oz
  • Angostura Bitters - 1 dash
  • Maraschino Cherry

Directions

  1. Fill glass with ice to chill
  2. Fill cocktail shaker with ice
  3. Add 2oz Rye Whiskey to the shaker
  4. Add .5oz Sweet Vermouth to the chilling glass
  5. Coat the glass in Sweet Vermouth
  6. Empty ice, Sweet Vermouth from chilling glass
  7. Stir contents and strain into chilled martini glass
  8. Garnish with a maraschino cherry
