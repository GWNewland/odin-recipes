Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Steps

  1. Make the Meat Sauce

    1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
    2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 Â½ hours, stirring occasionally.

  2. Cook the Noodles

    3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.

  3. Make the ricotta mixture

    4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.

  4. Layer the lasagna

    5. Self explanatory

  5. Cover with foil and bake

    6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
