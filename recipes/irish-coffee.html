Irish Coffee

Irish coffee is a delicious after-dinner cocktail. It's easy to make with freshly brewed coffee, Irish whiskey, and whipped cream. It's traditionally sweetened with brown sugar but Irish cream liqueur adds that little touch of luxury!

Ingredients

  • Irish Cream liquer - 1.5oz
  • Irish Whiskey - 1.5oz
  • Coffee - 1 cup, hot
  • Whipped Cream
  • Nutmeg - 1 dash (optional)

Directions

  1. Add 1.5oz Irish Whiskey to the mug
  2. Add 1.5oz Irish Cream liquer to the mug
  3. Add 1 cup hot coffee to the mug
  4. Top with whipped cream
  5. Garnish with nutmeg
